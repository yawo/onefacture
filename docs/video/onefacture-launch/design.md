# onefacture Launch Video Design Spec

## Art Direction
Fintech B2B, technical precision, authoritative but accessible.

## Palette
- **Ivory**: `#faf9f6` (Primary background)
- **Beige**: `#f5f2ec` (Secondary background/surface)
- **Chocolat**: `#3d2610` (Strong text)
- **Dark Brown**: `#1a1510` (Main text)
- **Accent**: `#7c4a1a` (Highlights)
- **Black**: `#000000` (Deep contrast)

## Typography
- **Display**: 'Zodiak', serif (for big, bold statements)
- **Body**: 'Satoshi', sans-serif (for details and technical copy)

## Motion Signature
- Clean, structured, mechanical.
- Precise transitions.
- Kinetic typography focusing on the "Unified API" message.
